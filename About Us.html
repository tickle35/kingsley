ABOUT US

History

Nestle Milo is from a Swiss multinational food and beverage company. The production of Milo in Ghana started in 971 at the Tema Factory

Mission

To nourish the Ghanaian youth by providing delicious and nutritious beverages that support the growth of the Ghanaian children and the youth.

Vision

Nestle Milo Ghana strive to be the leading beverage brand in the country.
